PBudget App Operation

Install the app onto your mobile devices. At the first launch, the app will take you to the "Settings" tab. The "Settings" tab lets add different multiple "Incomes and Expenses".

Under this "Settings" tab, you can perfectly add and edit your different incomes and expenses made in a particular time of the day. In the 'Expense', you can manually add in your different expenses made keeping in mind the different categories specified within the app. This was made so to all "Easy planning and Money allocation" to each particular category. Here you can also view your total Amount/Incomes, after which a particular Piechart portion will be generated depending on which amount has been allocated to each category in the "Graphs" section.

Once the information is entered, Data is Updated automatically within the system.
